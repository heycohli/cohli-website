Facilitate With Cohli

Hold the heart of a family cohort

Facilitators are the people who keep the social and emotional rhythm of a Cohli cohort alive. Not staff. Not therapists. Not hotel managers. The person who helps 6–12 families feel like a village.

See current openings How we work
What facilitating with us means

You hold the people, not the logistics.

Operations are handled by our team. Property is held by our hosts. Education is held by educators. You hold something subtler — the container that lets everyone arrive, soften, and find each other.

🌀

The rhythms

Opening Circle, weekly Parent Circles, closing ceremony. The structure is designed — you bring it to life with presence and care.

🪶

The conflict + repair

Tensions show up in any community. You catch them early, hold space honestly, and guide families toward repair rather than avoidance.

🤝

The belonging

You make sure every family — including the quieter ones, the late arrivals, the families with hard weeks — feels seen and woven in.

Two ways to facilitate with us

Pick the path that fits your life right now.

We're transparent about the exchange because we believe in real partnerships, not transactions. Both paths are real. Neither is better — they fit different seasons.

★ Preferred

Facilitating Family

A family that wants to live the Cohli experience and hold the community container. Your family participates fully — meals, accommodation, education for your kids, the whole experience — while you hold the relational space for the group.

The exchange: A significant discount on your family's participation fee (40–50%, discussed individually).

This is our preferred model. You're not "hired help" — you're a family in the village who also holds the heart of it. If you were already considering Cohli as a family, let's talk.

Facilitation Residency

An individual (or couple) — community facilitator, coach, retreat host, or aspiring coliving founder — who wants 4–6 weeks immersed in intentional family community.

The exchange: Accommodation and meals fully covered. No monetary compensation — this is a residency, not paid work.

You get weeks in an extraordinary place, a meaningful role, hands-on experience running an intentional coliving community, and a Cohli reference. Ideal for community facilitators between seasons.

Sobre Cohli

Cohli es una comunidad global de familias nómadas que co-crea experiencias de coliving y aprendizaje intencional alrededor del mundo. Este verano, realizamos nuestra segunda residencia en Casa Sumapaz, una propiedad espectacular en las montañas de Cundinamarca, Colombia — donde nuestra primera cohorte recibió un NPS de 9.77/10.

Buscamos a alguien que sea el corazón de esta cohorte: el/la Facilitador/a.

El Rol

Facilitador/a de Comunidad

Sostienes el contenedor social, emocional y cultural de 6–8 familias durante 4 semanas. No la logística (eso es operaciones), no la propiedad (eso es de los anfitriones), no la educación (eso es de los educadores). Tú sostienes a las personas.

  • Facilitar el Círculo de Apertura, los Círculos de Padres semanales y la ceremonia de cierre.
  • Establecer el ritmo y la energía del grupo. Invitar a la co-creación, no a la pasividad.
  • Detectar tensiones temprano y guiar los conflictos hacia la reparación.
  • Integrar a las familias que llegan de manera intencional; proteger la estabilidad del grupo.
  • Check-in semanal con los fundadores de Cohli (remoto) sobre la salud comunitaria y logros.

"No eres un/a monitor/a de campamento, ni terapeuta, ni gerente de hotel. Eres la persona que hace que 8 familias se sientan como una aldea."

About Cohli

Cohli is a global community of nomadic families cocreating intentional coliving and learning experiences around the world. This summer, we're hosting our second residency at Casa Sumapaz, a stunning property in the mountains of Cundinamarca, Colombia — where our first cohort scored 9.77/10 NPS.

We're looking for someone to be the heart of this cohort: the Facilitator.

The Role

Community Facilitator

You hold the social, emotional, and cultural container for 6–8 families over 4 weeks. Not the logistics (that's our ops team), not the property (that's our hosts), not the education (that's our educators). You hold the people.

  • Facilitate Opening Circle, weekly Parent Circles, and the closing ceremony.
  • Set the rhythm and energy of the group. Invite co-creation, not passivity.
  • Detect tensions early and guide conflict toward repair.
  • Integrate arriving families intentionally; protect the core group's stability.
  • Weekly check-in with Cohli founders (remote) on community health and wins.

"You are not a camp counselor, a therapist, or a hotel manager. You are the person who makes 8 families feel like a village."

The exchange: Accommodation and meals fully covered for the duration. No cash stipend — this is a facilitation residency, not a paid gig.

You get 4 weeks in the Colombian mountains, a meaningful role, hands-on experience running a coliving community, and a reference from Cohli. Great fit for experienced community builders or coaches between seasons.
